About UEM

The University of Engineering & Management, Kolkata founded by the IEM-Trust, has been established under the West Bengal Act XXV of 2014 passed by the State Legislature & published in Gazette notification on 21st January, 2015. It is one of the top most Universities in India under the able management of the IEM group which has been ranked as 79th All India ranking in NIRF by MHRD, Govt. Of India and 3rd in West Bengal,after IIT-Kharagpur and NIT-Durgapur in 2016. All Engineering and Management courses of the University are AICTE approved.
